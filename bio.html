With work experience in agriculture, customer service, and even trucking, I find user-centered design to be the field best suited to my vast range of interests.  My education in music and the liberal arts further instills a regard for how deep thinking, polished aesthetics, and exacting craftsmanship impact an audience for the better.

After completing a rigorous mentorship in user experience and user interface design, I am eager to join a team devoted to the iterative process of design.  I especially appreciate the user research, wireframing, prototyping, and user testing stages thanks to the crucial discoveries they afford for the benefit of users.
