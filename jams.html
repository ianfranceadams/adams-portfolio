“Bloc Jams” Front-end Development Project

This is a project I worked on in 2017 as part of Bloc.io's curriculum. The design of this music player was already decided upon, and I simply refined my basic knowledge of front-end web development as I worked on building it week by week using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

It proved to be a most humbling education in JavaScript and jQuery, keeping all the functions straight and making sure they got called when they were supposed to! I share this to show my level of exposure to these front-end technologies, but must admit they are not my main interest or forte by any means. I greatly respect the level of conscientiousness and capacity for abstraction that developers must exhibit, and am glad, as a designer, to be acquainted with what they contend with on a daily basis.
